# Memory Vault

Source: migrated from `chris-lej/school-saga-1` commit `a9a1a8fd8c27de849729fb98497fcfbc8dfd52de`

Purpose: preserve memories in their original form before adapting them into mechanics, scenes, characters, or fiction.

## Capture rules

- Record the memory before evaluating feasibility.
- Preserve sensory detail, emotional truth, uncertainty, and contradictions.
- Separate what definitely happened from what may be reconstructed or embellished by memory.
- Do not publish identifying details of real people without deliberate review.
- A memory does not need to become content to remain valuable.

## Seed memories and observations

### MEM-001: Ordinary Brazilian morning

**Source type:** direct environmental memory

A teenager leaves home in the morning. Someone nearby is washing the sidewalk. Dogs bark behind the gates of houses. Traffic, birds, voices, walls, utility poles, overhead wires, uneven sidewalks, and practical neighborhood construction create a strong sense of place.

**Emotional truth:** a normal day feels full of possibility.

**Possible adaptations:** opening soundscape, environment art direction, interactive neighborhood details.

**Complexity guess:** low to medium.

### MEM-002: The living school courtyard

**Source type:** direct memory

During recess, many groups of students occupy the courtyard at once. Sports, jokes, gossip, awkwardness, solitude, friendship, and small dramas overlap.

**Emotional truth:** school feels like a dense world of people and possibilities.

**Possible adaptations:** vertical-slice anchor scene, ambient NPC routines, basketball, rumors, social observation.

**Complexity guess:** high.

### MEM-003: Fear created by rumor

**Source type:** direct emotional memory

A place becomes frightening because students say it is haunted or strange. The fear may matter more than whether the rumor is objectively true.

**Emotional truth:** childhood imagination transforms ordinary spaces.

**Possible adaptations:** rumor system, time-dependent secret, lighting shift, exaggerated emotional presentation.

**Complexity guess:** medium.
